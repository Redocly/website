# Signup process

## Signup

Visit our [signup form](https://auth.cloud.redocly.com/registration) to get started.
If you use the email/password signup option, you will receive an email to verify your email address.


## Create an organization

When you first login, you are prompted to create an organization.
The organization creation starts a free trial (for 30 days).

## Create a project

You'll have the option to select from a few template options.
Our favorite template to start with is the "Warp Multi-sidebar" for moderate complexity technical doc projects.


## Subscribe

You may subscribe at any time during your trial period.
Visit the billing tab, configure your product, plan, and preferred billing period selections.
Enter your billing details, and get started.
You may invite someone else to complete the billing process.

Paying by invoice is only available on the Enterprise+ plans.
